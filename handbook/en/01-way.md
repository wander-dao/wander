# I · The Way

🌐 [中文](../01-總綱.md)

Five things, no more.

| Thing | From | To |
|-------|------|-----|
| **靈氣 Qi** | Every token through the terminal, all four classes (input / output / cacheWrite / cacheRead) | Pools in the Qi Sea |
| **氣海 Qi Sea** | The vessel; it has a limit | Overflows when full |
| **修為 Cultivation** | What you refine; never decreases | Sets the realm |
| **境界 Realm** | The rank Cultivation maps to | 練氣 and up |
| **靈石 Stones** | Compressed pool Qi, four grades | Into the bag; can flow back |

Two observation axes stand outside the game:

| Axis | Counts |
|------|--------|
| **薪火 Kindling** | Mortal cost, in silver taels — one tael is one US dollar |
| **靈力 Mana** | The 5-hour quota remaining, reported by the terminal |

## Two laws

- **Qi is not Cultivation.** Qi is total inflow; Cultivation is what you refined — never more.
- **Cultivation is not Realm.** One is a quantity; the other, the rank it maps to.

So the realm hangs not on lifetime totals but on what you refined. Refine it all, and the two meet — see [Reset](04-realm.md).

## One loop

```
terminal tokens ══ Qi
        │ pools
        ▼
     Qi Sea ─── full & untended → lost to the Cold Moon
        │
   ┌────┴────┐
 refine    compress
   ▼           ▼
Cultivation  Stones ──(decompress: back to the pool)
   ▼
 Realm
```

Refined Qi is set — only a reset re-deals it. Stones stay liquid — decompress returns them. **Push the realm, or hoard the stones** — that is the game.

**Q:** cacheRead is cheap; why does it count as Qi?
**A:** The measure is flow — whatever passes the terminal is practice. Money has its own axis, Kindling; the two never mix.
