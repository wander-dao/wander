# III · Spirit Stones

🌐 [中文](../03-靈石.md)

One low-grade stone is a million Qi. Adjacent grades run 100:1; a full hundred carries upward on its own.

| Grade | In low-grade | In Qi |
|-------|-------------|-------|
| 下品 low | 1 | 1M |
| 中品 mid | 100 | 100M |
| 上品 high | 10,000 | 10B |
| 極品 peak | 1,000,000 | 1T |

Compress to mint: `wander compress` — defaults to the whole pool.
Decompress to release: `wander decompress <count>` — the pool takes what it can hold; nothing is wasted.

Today stones are a store of value; Daoist titles, oracle redraws and artifact-forging are on the road. A stone banked now spends later. Change of heart? Decompress and refine — or simply [reset](04-realm.md).
