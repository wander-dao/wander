## The nine realms

練氣 Qi Refining, 築基 Foundation, 金丹 Golden Core, 元嬰 Nascent Soul, 化神 Spirit Transformation, 煉虛 Void Refining, 合體 Integration, 大乘 Mahayana, 渡劫 Tribulation — nine realms, and above them the immortal tiers.

Thresholds run ten-fold per realm: 築基 at 10B, 金丹 at 100B, 元嬰 at 1T, and on. Nine layers to a realm — 1–3 low, 4–6 mid, 7–8 high, 9 the peak. Within a layer, fast early and slow late: entering feels rewarding; the breakthrough asks for patience.

## Calibration

Thresholds count all four token classes — cacheRead is cheap, but it is Qi. Realm and dollars are deliberately decoupled: a high realm need not cost much. For real cost, read Kindling.

To check: `wander stats --realm`; the statusline's 【realm · layer · %】 shows in-layer progress.

## Reset

Lifetime tokens, re-dealt into one thing:

| Command | Effect |
|---------|--------|
| `wander reset` | All into **Cultivation** — the realm reflects lifetime at once; pool and stones cleared |
| `wander reset --to stones` | All into **Stones** — Cultivation zeroed; the sub-stone remainder returns to the pool |

Fully reversible; nothing is ever lost by it. It counts from lifetime, so Qi once scattered to the Cold Moon is counted back in — the only redemption there is.

**Q:** Why does the realm climb slowly?
**A:** What pools is only Qi; unrefined, it never becomes Cultivation. Refine often, or delegate to autoMode; to reflect all history at once, reset.
