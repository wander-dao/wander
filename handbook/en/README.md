## Three breaths

- One: everything through the terminal becomes Qi and pools in the Qi Sea; refine it into Cultivation or compress it into Stones — your call.
- Two: the Sea has a limit. Full and untended, Qi scatters to the Cold Moon. Rather not tend it? `wander config set autoMode cultivate`.
- Three: everything is local; the one thing worth backing up is `~/.local/share/wander/`.
